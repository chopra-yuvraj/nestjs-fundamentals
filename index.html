About TuteDude

TuteDude is a revolutionary learning platform designed to make programming and web development accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide high-quality education through interactive tutorials, hands-on projects, and a supportive community.

We believe that learning should be:

  • Interactive: Engage with live coding examples and real-time feedback
  • Practical: Build actual projects from day one
  • Supportive: Connect with mentors and peers in our community
  • Affordable: Access quality education without breaking the bank

Fun Facts & Trivia

Did you know? Here are some interesting facts about web development and learning:

Interesting Fact #1

The first website ever created was http://info.cern.ch/, built by Tim Berners-Lee in 1989 at CERN. It's still online today!

Interesting Fact #2

JavaScript was created in just 10 days by Brendan Eich in 1995, and it's now one of the most popular programming languages in the world.

Interesting Fact #3

Over 5 billion devices are estimated to be connected to the internet today. Web development skills are more valuable than ever!

Interesting Fact #4

CSS stands for Cascading Style Sheets, and the first version was released in 1996 by Håkon Wium Lie. Modern CSS is incredibly powerful and flexible.
